GirlsKnowGirls is a nonprofit organization focused on providing women with a safe online space. One-on-one chats are anonymous and private, allowing women to express themselves freely without external pressure. Discussions can range from, family, friends, and hobbies, to romantic relationships and mental health. Our goal is to make emotional well-being a priority in every woman's life.
